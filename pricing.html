Pricing FAQ

Will the Free tier ever be downgraded?

No. Whatever Free does today — full 7-layer verdict, AI Summary, Holder Activity, all of it — stays in Free forever. The only constraint we’re adding is the 50 scans/day cap, announced upfront, applied from CWS launch day. We’re not in the business of shipping a generous free tier and clawing it back six months later.

Why 50 scans per day specifically?

Casual users (the 95% majority) average 5-10 scans per day. 50 covers a heavy research session without hitting the cap. Power users who scan 100+ a day — usually traders trading real size — will run out and convert. That’s the segmentation: free for users, Pro for users whose tooling is part of their P&L.

Why announce Pro now if it doesn’t exist yet?

Two reasons. First, transparency — you should know on day one what the business model is, not discover it in a surprise update. Second, the waitlist gives early supporters a price advantage that wouldn’t be possible after public launch. Pro v1 launches in June 2026 with only the features listed above — we won’t bill anyone for vapor.

What does Lifetime actually mean?

Pay $149.99 USDC once, get every Pro feature for as long as Antares exists, including everything in Pro v2, v3, v4 (alerts, charts, push, smart-money, portfolio tracker). Lifetime is capped at 1000 supporters — once full, the 30-day pass is the only option. Lifetime members also get a private Discord channel, founder badge, and roadmap vote.

Can I get a refund?

30-day Pro pass: yes, within 7 days if you haven’t actively used the Pro features. Lifetime: yes, within 30 days. After that, Lifetime is non-refundable because the spot is removed from the 1000-cap inventory. Refunds are processed manually by sending USDC back to the wallet that paid — we use the on-chain transaction signature as the link, never an email or stored PII. Email lennypierrepro@gmail.com with the tx signature to request a refund.

Will there be student / underdog pricing?

Yes. Verifiable students and developers contributing to open source get 50% off Pro Monthly. Reach out via the waitlist with your situation and we’ll set it up.
