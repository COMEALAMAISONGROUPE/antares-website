Pricing FAQ

What does Free actually include?

The verdict, the score, and the 25 daily scans — nothing more. SAFE / CAUTION / DANGER / RUG label, 1000-point score under the hood, and the basic checks you see in the overlay (Sell, Mint, Freeze, LP, Liquidity). The deep-dive layers — AI Summary, Critical Flags panel, Full Analysis page (Holder Activity, Verdict Timeline, Critical Actors, insider clustering, wash-trading detection) — are Pro features. Free is the taste-test. Pro is the trading tool.

Why is Free so minimal?

Because we’re not running a charity. Free covers the cost of letting people sample the engine on a few tokens before they trust it with a payment. If you’re trading new launches every day, the verdict alone isn’t enough — you’ll want to see why a token got flagged, the AI summary that translates the math, and the full holder/insider analysis. Those features cost real compute and external API quota to deliver, so they’re paid. $24.99 / 30 days for unlimited everything is genuinely cheap if your tooling is part of your P&L.

Why 25 scans per day specifically?

If you’re actually trading, you’ll burn through 25 scans before lunch. The cap exists to mark the line between casual research and active trading: enough scans to evaluate the rug-detection on a few coins you’re curious about, not enough to run a full session of new launches. If your edge depends on this tooling, $24.99 / 30 days for unlimited is the only sensible move.

Why announce Pro now if it doesn’t exist yet?

Two reasons. First, transparency — you should know on day one what the business model is, not discover it in a surprise update. Second, the waitlist gives early supporters a price advantage that wouldn’t be possible after public launch. Pro v1 launches in June 2026 with only the features listed above — we won’t bill anyone for vapor.

What does Lifetime actually mean?

Pay $149.99 once (USDC or SOL, your call), get every Pro feature for as long as Antares exists, including everything in Pro v2, v3, v4 (alerts, charts, push, smart-money, portfolio tracker). Lifetime is capped at 1000 supporters — once full, the 30-day pass is the only option. Lifetime members also get a private Discord channel, founder badge, and roadmap vote.

Can I get a refund?

30-day Pro pass: yes, within 7 days if you haven’t actively used the Pro features. Lifetime: yes, within 30 days. After that, Lifetime is non-refundable because the spot is removed from the 1000-cap inventory. Refunds are processed manually by sending USDC back to the wallet that paid — we use the on-chain transaction signature as the link, never an email or stored PII. Email lennypierrepro@gmail.com with the tx signature to request a refund.

Will there be student / underdog pricing?

Yes. Verifiable students and developers contributing to open source get 50% off Pro Monthly. Reach out via the waitlist with your situation and we’ll set it up.

One bad bag costs more than Pro.

If you’re trading new launches without rug detection, you’re not trading — you’re donating. $24.99 for 30 days of unlimited scans, or $149.99 once for the rest of the project’s life. Pay direct in USDC or SOL, on-chain, no middleman, no card, no KYC. The next rug you avoid pays for the year.
