Core Features
⏱

Time-to-Rug Ring

Acute urgency, displayed as a countdown.

For RUG and DANGER verdicts the hero shows a circular ring with the median time-to-rug for comparable launches (e.g. 4h 12m). No competitor turns the verdict label into an operational deadline. Indicative — based on a heuristic profile match against pump.fun-style historical clusters.

👤

Critical Actors

Who holds what, in 3 cards.

Dev wallet reputation (prior tokens launched, prior rugs), insider sniper position, coordinated cluster size and coordination score. Each card surfaces the structural risk vector behind the score, not just a number.

🔄

Holder Activity (live)

See dev/whales dumping in real time.

Last-60-minute movement classification per top holder: Holding · Selling · Splitting · Reducing · Buying · Static. Net-flow footer (e.g. -3.2% pre-dump signature). Catches what static screenshots miss.

📋

Verdict Timeline

Watch the verdict trajectory, not a snapshot.

Per-token history of verdict changes across recent scans (CAUTION → DANGER → RUG with timestamps and the event that triggered the shift). Persisted server-side; you see the story of a token, not a frozen frame.

🎯

5-dimension Score Radar

Score broken down, not abstract.

The 1000-point composite score is decomposed across five axes: LP Security · Holder Distribution · Trading Authenticity · Token Maturity · Source Consensus. SVG pentagon radar shows where a token actually fails — not a single gauge.

🛡️

6-Source Consensus

No single source can lie alone.

DexScreener, RugCheck, GoPlus, Helius, Solscan and our in-house Chart engine. Geometric mean of 6 layers means a single false-clean source can’t override real risk — and a single false-positive can’t flip a healthy token to RUG.

What Else You Get
Source Breakdown

One row per upstream

RugCheck, Helius, Solscan, Chart Engine, DexScreener — each with its own derived verdict (OK / Risk / Flagged / N/A) and a one-line note. Open the accordion at the bottom of any analysis to see exactly which source said what. Forensic provenance, not a black-box score.

Outcome Histogram

Where this token sits in the distribution

36-bucket histogram showing how comparable launches died across 0h → 30d+. The current token’s expected position is marked with a YOU bar. Plus three most-similar past launches with their actual losses. Indicative model — useful as context, not forecast.

Exit Liquidity Calculator

How much can you actually sell

Slippage tiers from $100 to $20K against the live LP, computed with the constant-product AMM formula. Color-coded: green for safe exits, yellow for tax, red for “you’d crash the price”. Tells you the size you can realistically dump before you ape in.

AI Verdict Synthesis

Plain English, not jargon

Gemini-powered explanation of why the verdict landed where it did, in 2-4 sentences. References the actual data: dev wallet history, holder concentration, last-hour flow. Explains the mechanism (“coordinated wallets bought together at launch and now control a large share”), not just the verdict.

Foldable Sections

Read at your pace

Critical Flags, AI Verdict, Security strip, Critical Actors, Deep Analysis, Source Breakdown — every section is independently collapsible. Power users keep everything open; quick scans collapse to just the verdict + critical flags.

Universal Coverage

Wherever a Solana address appears

Activates on Twitter, Telegram, Discord, every Solana DEX (DexScreener, pump.fun, Axiom, Photon, Birdeye, GeckoTerminal, GMGN). Detects the address, runs the scan, surfaces the verdict before you can click buy.
